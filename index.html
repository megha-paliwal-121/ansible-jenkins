About Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced sport that requires agility, speed, and precision. It is played with a racket and a shuttlecock and can be played in singles or doubles format.

Rules of the Game

Badminton has specific rules regarding scoring, serving, and gameplay. Familiarize yourself with these rules to improve your game and enjoy it to the fullest.
